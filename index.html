Our Story

Born from a passion
for pure brilliance

Founded by Chirag Puri, Maahiraa Diamonds is a fine jewellery atelier rooted in the belief that a truly exquisite piece transcends time. Born from a deep reverence for the art of goldsmithing, every design is an expression of emotion - refined, intentional, and deeply personal.

We work exclusively with IGI, GIA, and HRD certified diamonds set in the finest 14K and 18K gold, collaborating with master craftsmen to bring each vision to life. We also offer fully custom-designed pieces crafted to your unique vision. From intimate solitaires to bold statement necklaces, Maahiraa Diamonds is where artistry meets authenticity.
