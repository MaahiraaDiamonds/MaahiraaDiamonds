Crafted with intention

Where trust meets timeless artistry.

Maahiraa Diamonds brings together meticulous craftsmanship, responsibly selected materials, and a refined eye for contemporary luxury. Each piece is designed to carry presence without excess, made for clients who value purity, detail, and quiet distinction.

From daily signatures to ceremonial statements, our jewellery is created with the care of an atelier and the assurance of a trusted fine jewellery house.

Founded by Chirag Puri, Maahiraa Diamonds is built on a personal commitment to quality, integrity, and the belief that exceptional jewellery should feel made for you.
